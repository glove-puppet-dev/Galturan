Galturan LLC provides a full-cycle operating platform for international suppliers entering and developing the Turkmenistan market. The platform is built on 25 years of local experience and combines market-entry planning, supplier relations, import coordination, logistics, compliance support, distribution, brand development, procurement, and financial coordination.

The company supports partners that need practical execution, not only market advice. Each capability is connected to the next stage of the commercial chain, from product assessment and documentation to delivery, buyer access, sales activation, and long-term development.

## Market Entry and Supplier Growth

Galturan LLC helps international manufacturers and brand owners enter Turkmenistan with a structured commercial model based on category potential, product readiness, local demand, price position, and distribution feasibility.

- Market-entry platform combining supplier relations, import operations, retail access, trade marketing, and sales development
- Direct cooperation with manufacturers, exporters, brand owners, and international trading partners
- Product portfolio development based on local demand, category potential, pricing strategy, quality standards, and retail performance
- Trade consulting and practical market strategy for pricing, category planning, retail readiness, distribution model planning, and market potential assessment
- Cooperation model selection: direct supply, distribution partnership, category development, procurement participation, or long-term brand representation
- Review of commercial terms, product documentation, product range, packaging, and launch requirements before market entry

## Import, Logistics, and Compliance

The company manages the operational path from global production centers to legal and efficient market availability in Turkmenistan. This includes coordination across suppliers, freight partners, customs processes, local requirements, and distribution planning.

- Global sourcing and logistics coordination through structured freight, delivery, and supply processes
- Import documentation, customs procedures, conformity requirements, labeling, packaging adaptation, and market-readiness support
- Coordination of product requirements, legal obligations, conformity assessment, certification, and regulatory documentation
- Warehousing, stock control, replenishment coordination, product availability management, and reliable supply for retail and wholesale channels
- Planning for repeat supply, product availability, stock movement, demand timing, and replenishment discipline
- Support for category-specific documentation and product-readiness requirements before launch

## Retail, Brand, and Sales Execution

Galturan LLC turns imported products into sustainable commercial performance across retail, wholesale, partner, specialized, and institutional channels. The company supports launch planning and ongoing sales execution rather than treating import as the final step.

- Structured access to the Turkmenistan consumer market through retail stores and partner sales channels
- Cooperation with business customers, specialized retail chains, regional wholesalers, affiliated platforms, and professional buyers
- Market positioning, launch planning, category placement, promotional strategy, consumer communication, and long-term brand growth
- Retail promotions, in-store visibility, merchandising coordination, campaign planning, product education, and sales activation
- Sales analytics, demand forecasting, category performance data, stock movement analysis, and pricing support
- Sales coverage across major cities, regional markets, retail outlets, and specialized sales channels
- Support for assortment planning, category placement, retail readiness, product education, and post-launch improvement
- Long-term brand-development work based on sales performance, buyer feedback, and market response

## Public Procurement and Financial Support

Galturan LLC supports complex commercial, institutional, and government procurement projects with documentation, compliance, logistics, delivery, and financing coordination. This capability is important for suppliers that want to participate in structured B2B or B2G supply in Turkmenistan.

- Participation in and execution of government tenders across various sectors
- Management of tender procedures, extensive documentation, strict delivery timelines, legal obligations, local certification, and large-scale purchasing projects
- Trade financing, banking instruments, payment coordination, structured procurement planning, and scalable commercial finance mechanisms
- Support for stable brand presence, regional expansion, sales growth, and sustainable business development in Turkmenistan
- Coordination between supplier requirements, buyer specifications, local documentation, delivery commitments, and payment structure
- Practical support for complex projects where timing, compliance, documentation, and supply reliability matter equally

## End-To-End Coordination

The value of Galturan LLC is in connecting these capabilities into one operating system. A supplier can work with the company on product assessment, import planning, compliance coordination, logistics, distribution, buyer access, procurement support, and brand growth through a single local partner with long-term knowledge of the Turkmenistan market.
