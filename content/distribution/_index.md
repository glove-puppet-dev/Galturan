Distribution is a central part of the Galturan LLC platform. The company helps products move from international suppliers into suitable local channels with structured coordination, practical market understanding, and 25 years of operating experience in Turkmenistan.

The distribution role begins before a product reaches the market. Galturan LLC reviews product fit, category readiness, documentation, import requirements, logistics conditions, pricing structure, retail expectations, and channel potential. This helps suppliers avoid fragmented execution and build a distribution model that can support repeat supply and long-term growth.

## Integrated Route To Market

1. Supplier negotiations and cooperation model planning
2. Product portfolio review and market-entry assessment
3. Global sourcing and procurement coordination
4. Freight, logistics, and import documentation
5. Customs coordination, conformity, and certification support
6. Warehousing, inventory planning, and replenishment control
7. Retail, wholesale, partner, and institutional distribution
8. Marketing, merchandising, trade execution, and sales activation
9. Analytics, buyer feedback, category improvement, and regional growth

## Distribution Channels

- Retail stores and sales channels
- Wholesale customers
- Partner networks
- Specialized retail chains
- Affiliated sales platforms
- Professional market segments
- Institutional and public-sector supply channels

## Channel Development

Galturan LLC supports channel development based on product type, target buyer, price segment, demand pattern, and supplier objectives. Some products are suitable for retail and consumer-facing trade marketing. Others require B2B distribution, wholesale cooperation, professional buyer access, institutional procurement, or public-sector project coordination.

The company helps align the product with the right route to market, including launch timing, buyer communication, promotional planning, replenishment logic, product education, and category placement.

## Retail and Wholesale Execution

Retail and wholesale distribution require more than product delivery. Galturan LLC supports visibility, merchandising, stock availability, category placement, promotional coordination, pricing support, and buyer follow-up. These details help supplier brands build consistent local presence rather than short-term availability.

## Regional and Institutional Supply

For regional markets, professional segments, and institutional supply, the company focuses on reliability, documentation, product availability, delivery timing, and clear coordination between supplier, buyer, logistics, and procurement requirements.

The distribution approach is built around reliable execution, clear communication, category performance, and sustainable growth for partner brands in Turkmenistan.
