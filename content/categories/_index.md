Galturan LLC works with diverse consumer product categories and supports brands that are ready to enter or expand in the Turkmenistan market. Category development is guided by 25 years of local operating experience, practical knowledge of buyer expectations, and the ability to connect import readiness with distribution and sales execution.

The category portfolio can expand based on supplier opportunities, market demand, compliance requirements, pricing strategy, quality standards, product documentation, shelf readiness, and distribution potential.

## Core Categories

- Food & Beverages
- Household Goods
- Personal Care
- Baby & Kids Products
- Electronics
- General Consumer Goods

## Category Development

Product portfolio development is based on local demand, category potential, pricing structure, quality standards, retail readiness, documentation, certification requirements, logistics conditions, and performance across distribution channels.

Galturan LLC supports assortment planning, launch preparation, market positioning, product education, trade marketing, merchandising, replenishment planning, and long-term category growth for suitable products.

## Category Selection Criteria

Before a category or supplier cooperation model is developed, the company reviews several practical factors:

- Product quality, shelf life, packaging, and presentation
- Documentation, certification, labeling, and conformity requirements
- Pricing structure and competitiveness for the Turkmenistan market
- Demand potential across retail, wholesale, B2B, and institutional channels
- Logistics complexity, storage conditions, and replenishment timing
- Supplier reliability, production capacity, and export readiness
- Marketing support, product education, and long-term brand potential

## Consumer Goods Focus

For food and beverage products, the company evaluates import requirements, shelf-life discipline, product rotation, packaging adaptation, price positioning, and channel fit. For household goods and personal care products, the focus expands to merchandising, consumer communication, quality standards, and repeat-purchase potential.

For baby and kids products, product safety, documentation, packaging clarity, and trusted distribution are especially important. For electronics and general consumer goods, Galturan LLC reviews technical documentation, warranty expectations, product education, buyer support, and the ability to maintain stable supply.

## Long-Term Category Growth

Galturan LLC does not treat category work as a one-time listing exercise. The company supports long-term category development through market feedback, sales performance monitoring, replenishment coordination, buyer communication, and practical improvements to the product range.
