Galturan LLC is a Turkmenistan-based commercial platform with 25 years of operating experience in the local market. The company supports international suppliers, manufacturers, brand owners, exporters, and trading partners that need a practical route into Turkmenistan through import operations, logistics, compliance coordination, distribution, procurement support, retail access, and brand development.

The company is positioned as a local operating partner rather than a narrow intermediary. Its role is to connect international supply with the real requirements of the Turkmenistan market: product readiness, documentation, customs coordination, certification support, warehousing, distribution planning, buyer communication, trade execution, and long-term category growth.

## Company Profile

Galturan LLC works at the intersection of international supplier relations and local commercial execution. Over 25 years in Turkmenistan, the company has built practical knowledge of how products move from supplier negotiations to local market availability, how buyer expectations differ by category, and how import, distribution, and procurement projects need to be coordinated to remain reliable.

The company supports both commercial and institutional routes to market. This includes consumer goods distribution, wholesale and partner-channel sales, specialized retail cooperation, B2B supply, B2G procurement support, tender-related coordination, and long-term brand development.

## What Galturan LLC Does

Galturan LLC helps international partners understand and execute the operational chain required for market entry:

- Market-entry assessment and cooperation model planning
- Supplier negotiations and product portfolio review
- Import documentation and customs coordination
- Certification, conformity, labeling, and packaging-readiness support
- Freight, logistics, warehousing, inventory, and replenishment coordination
- Retail, wholesale, partner-channel, and institutional distribution
- Trade marketing, merchandising, launch planning, and sales activation
- Public-sector procurement, tender support, and complex project coordination
- Banking, payment, trade finance, and structured procurement coordination

## A Vertically Integrated Commercial Platform

Galturan LLC manages the complete chain of commercial operations so suppliers do not need to coordinate disconnected local providers for each stage of market entry. The operating model is built around one connected workflow:

- Supplier negotiations and sourcing coordination
- Procurement planning and product portfolio development
- Freight, logistics, customs coordination, and certification support
- Warehousing, inventory planning, and product availability management
- Retail placement, wholesale distribution, and partner-channel access
- Marketing, trade execution, merchandising, and brand development
- Sales analytics, category performance monitoring, and regional growth

## Market Role

Galturan LLC helps suppliers move from general market interest to practical local execution. The company combines local market knowledge with operational discipline, helping partner brands define realistic entry models, prepare products for local requirements, reach suitable buyers, and build a stable commercial presence in Turkmenistan.

The market role is especially important for suppliers that need more than simple import handling. Product categories often require careful positioning, documentation review, price alignment, distribution planning, replenishment discipline, and ongoing communication with buyers. Galturan LLC supports these details as part of the full-cycle platform.

- Brand owners and exporters

## Operating Principles

Galturan LLC works around practical execution, long-term reliability, and clear coordination. The company prioritizes categories and partnerships where a supplier's product quality, documentation, commercial model, and market potential can support sustainable growth in Turkmenistan.

The company is especially suitable for partners that need a local team capable of coordinating market-entry strategy, import operations, buyer communication, retail and wholesale execution, and procurement-related requirements in one structured process.
